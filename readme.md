# His Majesty's Factory Expansion Act

A Factorio overhaul mod where the colony has been issued a Royal Charter. Tea is mandatory, exoskeletons are forbidden, and victory is announced by Michael Caine.

## How it works

1. Standard Factorio start.
2. From the first tick a craving timer ticks down: **Satiated → Craving → Craven**. Eat **Cuppa Tea** or **Fish & Chips** to reset it. Reach Craven and your factory effectively shuts down.
3. Climb a re-themed tech tree: Longbow → Musket → Wand → Spells → Philosopher's Stone → Truthbomb → Mr. Blobby. The Truthbomb (NHS Double Decker Bus) is the final research before the Rocket Silo.
4. The vehicles are British: Mini Cooper, Challenger / Churchill tank (which loops "God Save the King" — a deliberately bad recording), and the Rocket Silo as the EU Headquarters.
5. Win by firing **Mr. Blobby** into space. Flip the runtime Mr. Blobby setting **off mid-game** to skip the win condition and earn the **You Whimp** achievement (the achievement only fires on an in-game setting flip, not on a fresh-start choice).

## Highlights

- **Cuppa Tea** is hand-craft only and can spoil. A spoiled cup sends you to the Tower of London for one minute.
- **Fish & Chips** is the buildable alternative — fish (hand-caught or greenhouse) plus chips (woodchipper, wood + greenhouse potatoes).
- **Exoskeleton** kills the player on equip. _"British Officers don't run."_
- **NHS Medkit** — free to craft, 30-minute craft time, 30–90 second activation delay, full heal.
- **Biters** are reskinned as Rambo soldiers; nests become Barrack Tents and EU Buildings; dead biters drop EU Flags that take 30 seconds to hand-mine and cannot be bot-mined.
- **Truthbomb (NHS Double Decker Bus)** — final pre-rocket tech, massive explosion, plays Michael Caine's _"You were only supposed to blow the bloody doors off."_
